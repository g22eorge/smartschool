Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page.

What should I do if I can't scan the QR code?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and that your camera has permission to access the QR code scanner. If the issue persists, please inform your lecturer.

How can I view my attendance history?

Navigate to your dashboard where you can view your attendance history and statistics for each module.
